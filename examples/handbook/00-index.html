About this handbook

Every page in this handbook is a separate orfc plan, with its own slug, version history, and comment thread. They share a folder (handbook) and a tag (#handbook) so they group together in the dashboard.

Each page is HTML, not markdown. They're authored as standalone files in examples/handbook/ and pushed via orfc push <file>.html. Update one and re-push with --update <slug> — old versions stay accessible from the version-history sidebar.
